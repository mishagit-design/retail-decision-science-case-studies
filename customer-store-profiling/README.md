# Customer & Store Profiling Framework

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often have access to large volumes of customer transaction data but struggle to translate that information into actionable customer insights and store-level strategies.

## Objective

Develop customer and store profiling frameworks that support segmentation, personalization, and strategic decision-making.

## Approach

* Analyzed customer purchasing behavior across channels and time periods
* Created customer profile attributes based on transaction patterns
* Developed store-level behavioral profiles to understand localized demand
* Validated profile logic through SQL-based quality assurance processes
* Partnered with business stakeholders to ensure profiles aligned with merchandising and marketing objectives

## Technologies

* Python
* SQL
* BigQuery
* Customer Analytics
* Segmentation
* Behavioral Profiling

## Impact

* Enabled deeper understanding of customer behavior
* Supported personalization and customer targeting initiatives
* Improved visibility into store-specific customer preferences
* Provided a scalable framework for future segmentation projects

## Key Learnings

Effective personalization begins with understanding behavioral patterns at both the customer and store level. Data-driven segmentation creates opportunities for more relevant customer experiences and business decisions.
